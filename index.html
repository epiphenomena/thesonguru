Enlightened Song Editing

At TheSONGuru, we specialize in refining the raw material of your creativity into polished, impactful art.

We provide objective, technical, and artistic editing services for songwriters of all levels. Whether you are stuck on a bridge, struggling with prosody, or simply need a second pair of experienced ears, we offer the guidance needed to elevate your song and make it recording ready.

Our approach is simple: we respect the unique voice of your song and artistry while sharpening the message and delivery. We look at structure, rhyme schemes, melodic motifs, lyrical clarity and more to ensure your song is received exactly as you intend, and achieves maximum emotional connection with your listeners.

Who We Are

Aryn Michelle Calhoun is a professional singer/songwriter and has been editing songs for her peers, co-writers, students and clients for over 20 years. She has released acclaimed studio albums, EPs and singles and has degrees in music from Southwestern University and the Berklee College of Music. She has won numerous awards for songwriting including the Scott Benson Award, the Gospel Music Association's Immerse Award and has been a finalist/winner in songwriting competitions including the International Songwriting Competition, The Al Johnson Performing Songwriter Contest, and the SongDoor International Songwriting Competition.

Aryn is the Songwriting Division Head for Visible Music College and in 2026 released her first textbook on songwriting “PLAY, SHAPE, CREATE : The Joyful Art of Songwriting.”

Please Note: TheSONGuru is not a publishing company, a record label, a demo-making service, a co-writer or a radio promoter. Please only hire us if you want us to help you edit your songs to make them even better than they already are!
